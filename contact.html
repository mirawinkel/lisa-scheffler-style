Covid-19

Returning to work, and sharing a space in a salon seemed unsettling to my clients as well as myself. So I decided to create a private chic salon, in the comfort and safety of my home. I wanted to be able to provide a tranquil atmosphere where there is no worry about social distancing. My space has the top of the line air purifier. I also leave windows and doors open to provide airflow. We are required to both wear masks at all times.

I have been providing services in my new space since November. My guests are grateful for an intimate setting where they feel safe and are able to relax. They also love meeting our chickens!!

Please be mindful to arrive AT your appointment time. Please DO NOT be late, I must allow time between my guests to clean and sanitize.

I truly appreciate your support and I look forward to seeing you!!

Lisa Marie
