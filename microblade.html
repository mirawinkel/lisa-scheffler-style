What is microblading?

How does it work?

Using a microblade tool, pigment is deposited into the upper layers of the skin in hair-like incisions to enhance, reshape or fill in your eyebrows. The results give you natural looking eyebrows 24/7 with no effort, it's like an instant eyelift!
