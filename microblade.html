What is microblading?

How does it work?

Microblading is a new form of semi-permanent make-up for your eyebrows. It is a manual process performed with a handheld tool called a microblade.

Pigment is deposited into the upper layers of the skin in hair-like incisions to enhance, reshape or fill in your eyebrows. The results not only give you natural looking eyebrows, it's like an instant eyelift!
