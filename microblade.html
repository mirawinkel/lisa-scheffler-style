What is microblading?

" It's like an instant facelift, Without the downtime. "

-Lisa Marie Scheffler

Microblading is a technique that creates fine strokes that replicate the look of real hair

The Process

Microblading is a cosmetic technique where pigment is inserted into the skin to resemble makeup. It is typically achieved using a machine or manual hand tool.

Part 2

Microblading is at least a two part process. Patience is a virtue. I recommend a 6-8 week touch-up to refine the look.

How long will my microbladed brows last?

Cosmetic tattooing is considered semi permanent, the pigments are designed to fade over time. Factors such as skin type, pigment color and sun exposure can affect the longevity.
