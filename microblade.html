What is microblading?

" It's like an instant eyelift! Without the downtime. "

-Lisa Scheffler

How does it work?

Pigment is deposited into the upper layers of the skin in hair-like incisions to enhance, reshape or fill in your eyebrows.

The Process

Unlike traditional tattooing, microblading artists apply individual hair-strokes at a very shallow depth. This produces very fine hair-like lines that resemble real hair. Each individual hair-stroke can be blended in with the client’s existing eyebrow hair.

Part 2

Microblading is done in two parts. An initial session, then a follow up 6-8 weeks later to refine the look and finish the process

How long will my microbladed brows last?

Cosmetic tattooing is considered semi-permanent as it does not wash away, however the pigments are designed to fade over time. Factors such as skin type, pigment color and sun exposure can affect the rate that pigments are broken down. For best results it is recommended that you follow the aftercare instructions and touch-up your eyebrows every year.
